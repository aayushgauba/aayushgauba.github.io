          About

          I’m Aayush Gauba, a researcher and developer focused on building intelligent, adaptive systems. My work spans artificial intelligence, quantum-inspired computing, and applied security.

          I am the creator of AIWAF, an open-source web application firewall that uses machine learning to detect and block malicious activity in real time across multiple platforms.

          Alongside development, I actively contribute to the tech community as a speaker, organizer, and mentor. I’ve presented at major conferences including PyCon US and DjangoCon US, and I serve as a PyData St. Louis organizer and part of the DjangoCon US organizing team.

          My goal is to build systems that are not just efficient, but adaptive, secure, and capable of learning from real-world behavior.

          This site is intentionally lightweight so it loads fast and is easy to update.
